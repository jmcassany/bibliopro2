ePROVIDETM is a platform providing access to information on Clinical Outcome Assessments (COAs). Contained therein is PROQOLIDTM database created in 2002 by Mapi Research Trust to aid you in finding the instrument that is right for your study.It has grown exponentially over the years and today more than 1,400 COAs are comprehensively described, covering all generic or conditions specific domains and all populations.

In PROQOLIDTM, users are provided with two levels of information access:

- Free level which includes basic information to identify a COA: Full name of questionnaire & its acronym, Author(s), Copyright ownership, Objective, Disease(s), Questionnaire type, Original language.

- Member-Access level which includes detailed information about:

Review copies (original questionnaires, translations and user manuals), Contact information, Conditions of use, List of existing translations, Concept of interest, Population for intended use, Administration mode, Recall / Observation period, Domains covered, Scoring information, Content validity documentation, Measurement properties, Bibliographic references and Relevant links.  This level is accessible to any subscriber on a yearly basis.

For more information, please visit https://eprovide.mapi-trust.org/.
