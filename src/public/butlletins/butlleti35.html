Dear Dr. [[camp1]],

My name is Mireya and I am writing to you on behalf of Dr. Jordi Alonso from the Health Services Research Group of the IMIM-Research Institute Hospital del Mar in Barcelona, Spain.

We would like to request your permission to include the [[camp2]] Spanish version questionnaire in BiblioPRO.

We recently developed an on-line library of PRO questionnaires in Spanish (BiblioPRO), intended to promote appropriate use of the instruments included, and to serve as an educational tool for users (free usage for non-for profit studies), and we hope you have no objection to including the questionnaire in it.

The mission of our project is to promote the adequate use of PRO instruments in Spanish by providing accurate online information and guidance. The Scientific Committee of BiblioPRO is formed by the most experienced researchers in this field in Spain. BiblioPRO is funded by the Spanish Ministry of Science and Innovation.

Given the current proliferation of different versions of PRO instruments, we believe it is important to identify and classify all available, official Spanish versions of those instruments and to include all relevant information about their validity and applications in a user-friendly, Internet environment. In doing so, BiblioPRO emphasizes information about the authors of the original versions, as well as stressing that instrument use should comply with copyright laws.

An original added value of BiblioPRO will be the incorporation of evidence-based evaluations of the instruments included. We have developed a promising evaluation tool, EMPRO, which we intend to use to evaluate the conceptual and metric characteristics of all instruments in the database. (A reprint of the recent publication in Value in Health is attached.) BiblioPRO also provides a useful framework for managing the considerable amount of consultancy and educational activities we are involved in.

Due to its public funding, scientific robustness, service orientation, and our strict respect for copyright laws, we want that BiblioPRO becomes a very useful platform to help leading HRQL researchers further disseminate their scientific work and products.

You can access the information we have posted on the Spanish version of your questionnaire at this link [[link1]] . Although the information is only in Spanish (since this is the intended target user population), you will see that your contact address is clearly indicated.

I would really appreciate it if you could confirm your acceptance to include and reproduce the Spanish version of the [[camp2]] questionnaire via BiblioPRO.


I would be very glad to talk these issues over the phone if you need any further explanations, and I am available at this email address if you prefer. We hope to be able to include the [[camp2]] Spanish version in BiblioPRO pretty soon and make these instruments available to the Spanish scientific community.

Most cordially yours,

Mireya García-Durán
